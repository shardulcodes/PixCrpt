About PixCrypt

PixCrypt is a modern steganography tool designed to securely hide images within other files. Our goal is to provide a simple, fast, and reliable way to manage sensitive image content while maintaining a clean and intuitive user experience.

Key Features

  • Encrypt and decrypt images securely and efficiently.
  • Modern interface with real-time previews and progress tracking.
  • Supports multiple image formats with consistent quality.
  • Designed for ethical use and privacy-first operations.

Our Vision

We aim to make steganography accessible to everyone, combining powerful encryption techniques with an interface that is both beautiful and functional. Privacy and security are at the core of everything we build.

Developer
